# Score Win Bet Sum

Betting Strategy

The system determines the next bet based on the outcome of the previous hand.

Bet on the Streak (Previous Winner)

We bet on the streak if any of the following conditions are met:

  • Strong Win (S): The winner scored 8 or 9.
  • Bad Beat (B): The difference between the winner and loser scores is exactly 1 point (e.g., 9-8, 8-7).
  • Baccarat (Z): The loser scored 0.

Bet on the Opposite

If none of the above conditions are met, we bet on the opposite of the previous winner.
